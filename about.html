Artist, character designer, irl chao

If its weird and sold 100 copies in 2002 I probably love it. Inspired by works like Chulip, MoonRPG, Nights into Dreams, Digimon World, Spyro, and every single neopets petpage.

2013 I designed for TF2 and Dota2 in the Steam Workshop, creating official Valve merchandise with WeLoveFine. I also painted backgrounds for OK KO, Eddsworld, Homestuck, and Brawlhalla.

2016 onwards my focus has been concept art and character design! Projects like Holofist, Coruncopia, and the Square Enix Music channel mascot - Mr.SEM.

Most recently I spent over a year developing a game at Studio Thunderhorse, involved in fundamental decisions like "what if breaking rocks rarely reveals a lil bonus rat?"

Currently looking for long term work at game studios.
