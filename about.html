I'm an illustrator with an interest in games, character design, and promo material.

Past clients include Valve, Square Enix, Homestuck, Studio Yotta, Brawlhalla, Knight Club.

In 2015 I graduated from Pratt Institute's Communication Design program. During my college years I created merchandise with Valve and ForFansByFans for TF2 and Dota2. I then went on to paint backgrounds for animations like OK KO, Eddsworld, Homestuck, and Brawlhalla.

Recently I've been designing characters for games. I designed monsters, trading cards, and promo art for the upcoming game Holofist. I also created NPC's for another upcoming game, Cornucopia.

Looking forward to the next adventure!
